Cupping therapy is a form of pseudoscience in which a local suction is created on the skin with the application of heated cups. As alternative medicine, it is practiced primarily in Asia but also in Eastern Europe, the Middle East, and Latin America. Cupping therapy is an ancient healing method that may ease back pain, neck pain, headaches, and other issues. It uses suction to pull on your skin and increase blood flow to the affected area.
